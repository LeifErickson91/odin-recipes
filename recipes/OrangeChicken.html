Orange Chicken

Description

A classic of Chinese American food! Easy to make. Unhatable chicken lathered in Orange sauce

  • Chicken
  • Oragne Sauce
  • Rice
  • Broccolli
  1. Preheat the oven at 400 degress
  2. Lay the chciken out on a tray and put it in the oven
  3. Place the orange sauce in a bowl of warm water
  4. Microwave the rice for 4 minutes
  5. Microwave the brocoli for 4 minutes
  6. After the chicken has finished cooking, appy the orange sauce to it
  7. Mix everything together in a bowl
  8. Enjoy!
