Tomato Basil Mozzarella Sandwich

Description

This is my favorite sandwich! Fresh juicy tomatoes along with the subtle flavor and creaminess of Mozzarella chess. Add the invigorating flavor of basil and enjoy!

  • Bread
  • Pesto
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Mozzarella
  1. Cut the bread
  2. Spread the pesto
  3. Cut the Tomatos and lay them on the bread
  4. Add the basil
  5. Cut and lay the mozzarella
  6. Toast the whole sandwich
  7. Bon apetit!
